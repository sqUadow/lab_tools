How to use:   Click anywhere on a legend row to select that color, then click wells to paint them. Clicking a well that already uses the active color will clear it. Double-click a swatch to change its color, or double-click a label to rename it. Click a row letter (A–H) or column number (1–12) to fill that entire row/column. Use the Well Labels panel on the right to add per-well text annotations — Tab or Enter moves between fields. Labels appear below each well on the printed sheet.
